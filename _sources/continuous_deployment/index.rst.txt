#####################
Continuous Deployment
#####################

The topics in this section focus on the workflows and other automation
techniques used to ensure that changes to NWChemEx's repos happen in as
reliable of a manner as possible. Strictly speaking this automation covers
both merging the software into the master branch (continuous integration), and
deploying the master branch (continuous deployment).
